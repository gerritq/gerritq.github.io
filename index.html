Gerrit Michele Quaremba

I am an incoming PhD candidate in Computer Science funded by the UKRI Centre for Doctoral Training (CDT) in Safe and Trusted Artificial Intelligence, hosted by King’s College London. I am part of the Participatory Harm Auditing Workbenches and Methodologies (PHAWM) project, supervised by Dr Elizabeth Black and Professor Elena Simperl. Specifically, I work on the content collaboration use case, focusing on the design and evaluation of article generation models. I am grateful to receive additional support from the Kölner Gymnasial- und Stiftungsfonds.

Previously, I worked at Harvard as a Research Fellow and at Durham as a Research Assistant. I graduated with an MSc in Applied Social Data Science from the LSE and a BSc in Economics from Cologne.
