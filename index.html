Hi, I am an incoming PhD candidate in Computer Science funded by the UKRI Centre for Doctoral Training (CDT) in Safe and Trusted Artificial Intelligence, hosted by King’s College London. My am part of the Participatory Harm Auditing Workbenches and Methodologies (PHAWM) project, supervised by Dr. Elizabeth Black and Professor Elena Simperl. Specifically, I work on the content collaboration use case focusing on the design and evaluation of article generation models. I am grateful to receive additional support from the Kölner Gymnasial- und Stiftungsfonds

Previously, I worked at Harvard as a Research Fellow and Durham as a Research Assistant. I graduated with a MSc in Applied Social Data Science from the LSE, and a BSc in Economcis from Cologne.
